AEO Audit: Find Out Why AI Isn't Citing You

You rank. You have good content. But when people ask ChatGPT about your category, someone else gets recommended.

Our AEO Audit shows you exactly why — and what to fix.

In 12 dimensions, we analyze your citation readiness: technical setup, content structure, competitive positioning, and authority signals. You'll leave knowing who AI recommends instead of you, what they have that you don't, and the priority fixes to get cited.

The 12 Dimensions of Citation Readiness

Technical Foundations

Dimensions 1-4

  • 1. FAQ Schema — Implementation and extractability
  • 2. Product/Service Schema — AI-parseable structure
  • 3. Author/Expert Markup — Expertise attribution
  • 4. Technical Accessibility — Crawlability and speed

Content Structure

Dimensions 5-8

  • 5. Q&A Format — Structure vs. walls of text
  • 6. Direct Answers — First 1-2 sentence quality
  • 7. Citeable Statistics — Specific, attributable data
  • 8. Expert Voice — Named experts with credentials

Competitive Position

Dimensions 9-12

  • 9. Citation Status — Who AI recommends now
  • 10. Citation Share — You vs. competitors
  • 11. Content Gaps — Uncovered queries
  • 12. Authority Signals — E-E-A-T comparison
