How Our Fractional CMO Engagement Works

We follow a proven process refined through dozens of engagements.

1

Discovery and Assessment

Weeks 1-2

We immerse ourselves in your business with deep dives on goals, existing data, customer and competitive landscape, technology audit, and team assessment.

Output: Assessment report with prioritized recommendations and preliminary roadmap.

2

Strategic Planning

Weeks 3-4

We develop your marketing strategy including positioning framework, channel strategy, content strategy, campaign strategy, team strategy, and budget allocation.

Output: Comprehensive marketing strategy document and 90-day execution roadmap.

3

Execution and Optimization

Ongoing

Strategy without execution is worthless. We work alongside your team to implement priority campaigns, build systems, optimize based on results, and scale what works.

Output: Continuous improvement and measurable business outcomes.
