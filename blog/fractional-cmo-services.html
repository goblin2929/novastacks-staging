Fractional CMO Services — Senior Marketing Leadership, Flexible Engagement

Our fractional CMO services deliver executive marketing leadership without the full-time commitment. You get a senior marketing leader who embeds with your team, develops strategy, builds systems, and drives results — at a fraction of the cost of a full-time CMO hire.

But here's what makes us different: we don't just give you strategy and leave you to figure out execution.

We combine 20+ years of experience building growth systems at companies like Tencent and Expedia with AI-powered agentic workflows that enable us to execute at 10x the speed of traditional agencies. You get a fractional CMO who can actually do things, not just advise.

The companies we work with — Series A-C startups, growth-stage SMBs, companies in transition — need marketing leadership that delivers outcomes. Not more slide decks. Not more strategic frameworks. Actual results: more qualified leads, higher conversion rates, better unit economics.

What's Included in Our Fractional CMO Services

Every engagement is customized to your situation, but here's what you can expect:

Strategic Marketing Leadership

  • Marketing strategy development — Comprehensive plans aligned with business goals
  • Positioning and messaging — Clear articulation of value proposition
  • Channel strategy — Where to invest, where to cut, how to allocate budget
  • Go-to-market planning — Launch strategies for new products and markets

Team Building and Management

  • Role definition — What roles you need (and don't need) at your stage
  • Hiring strategy — In-house vs. agency vs. contractor decisions
  • Recruiting — Writing job descriptions, sourcing candidates, interviewing
  • Management — 1:1s, performance feedback, coaching

Marketing Operations and Systems

  • Tech stack selection — CRM, marketing automation, analytics, attribution
  • Process documentation — SOPs for repeatable marketing functions
  • Reporting dashboards — Real-time visibility into marketing performance

How Our Fractional CMO Engagement Works

We follow a proven process refined through dozens of engagements:

1

Discovery and Assessment (Weeks 1-2)

Deep dive with leadership, review existing marketing, customer and competitive assessment, technology audit, team assessment.

2

Strategic Planning (Weeks 3-4)

Positioning and messaging framework, channel strategy, content strategy, campaign strategy, team strategy, budget allocation.

3

Foundation Building (Month 2-3)

Priority campaigns launched, team hiring, systems implementation, process documentation, initial performance tracking.

4

Optimization and Scale (Month 4+)

Performance analysis, budget reallocation, new initiative launches, scaling what works, continuous optimization.

Agentic Workflows for 10x Faster Execution

We've built agentic marketing workflows that enable us to execute at 10x the speed of traditional approaches:

  • Content agents produce research, briefs, and drafts at unprecedented speed
  • Analytics agents deliver insights without manual data wrangling
  • Implementation agents execute technical changes that would take specialists weeks

The result: you get the strategic depth of a senior fractional CMO with the execution capacity of a full marketing team.
