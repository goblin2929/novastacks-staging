FAQ: Answer Engine Optimization Services

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring content to be directly cited by AI assistants like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overview. Unlike traditional SEO which targets search rankings, AEO optimizes for AI extraction and citation. Key elements include FAQ schema, Q&A content structure, expert attribution, and citeable statistics.

SEO optimizes for link clicks in search results. AEO optimizes for being the source AI uses to answer questions. While both improve organic visibility, they require different signals: SEO relies on backlinks and keyword optimization, while AEO requires FAQ schema, direct answers, and structured Q&A content. You need both to maintain visibility as search behavior shifts toward AI assistants. See detailed AEO vs SEO comparison.

Rankings and citations are different games. We've seen companies rank #1 for their core keyword but get zero AI citations — while a competitor on page two gets recommended by ChatGPT. Why? Because AI doesn't care about your PageRank. It cares about whether it can extract a clear, credible, well-structured answer. Answer Architecture ensures your SEO investment translates to AI visibility — not just search visibility.

Most clients see their first AI citations within 30-60 days of implementation. Achieving 15+ monthly citations typically takes 3-6 months, depending on content volume and competitive landscape. This is significantly faster than traditional SEO (which takes 6-12 months for ranking improvements).

SaaS, e-commerce, and B2B service providers see the strongest ROI from AEO. Any business where potential customers ask AI for recommendations, comparisons, or solutions can benefit. If your prospects are asking "best [category]" or "how to choose [solution]," AEO is critical.
